MFA Design & Technology Thesis, 2016

stickie.space

Stickie is a mobile device centric collaborative system developed by Jaskirat at Parsons School of Design,NY. The project utilizes physical space as a valuable medium for people to digitally annotate their environment. Stickie incorporates the smartphone as an extension of the human body to spatially interact with visual content.

Jaskirat is a designer, a developer and an engineer. He is a masters candidate in the Design & Technology graduate program at Parsons School of Design, New York. Jaskirat is also a research assistant and teaching assistant at Parsons.

He is focused on the forefront of emerging technologies in interaction design. Concurrently, he believes in designing with a conscience and responsibility.
