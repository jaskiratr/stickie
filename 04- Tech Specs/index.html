Technical Specifications

2016-02-21

Specifications for the final Thesis show.

  1. Space Requirement: 4’X7’

  2. Furniture and Build:

    • Equiment Rack/Podium
    • TV Ceiling Mounts - Available on Amazon

  3. Presentation technology:

    • Speakers for localized sound
    • 3x 4K Screens Available on Amazon
    • 2x Mobile Phones - Motorola Nexus 6

  4. Connectivity:
    Wifi Router with internet connection

  5. Electricity and lighting: Power strip with upto 6 outlets

  6. Audience Interaction: One by one with guided tutorial. Two at a time if possible.

  7. Participants: Atleast two people need to use it at the same time. Hence one actor will be present to fill in the spot of second person, whenever possible.

  8. Timing:

    • The demos will occur every 30 min
    • Individual demo will last less than 5 min.
    • The video will loop after 90 seconds.

  9. Signage: The countdown to next demo will be displayed on user screens.
